# Hadron

> A **hadron** is a particle which is affected by the [[strong nuclear force]].

## Quarks

Hadrons are made up of 2-3 [[quark]]s.

## Types of hadron

- [[baryon]]
    - [[proton]]
    - [[neutron]]
- [[meson]]s
    - [[pion]]
    - [[kaon]]
