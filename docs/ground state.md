# Ground state

The *bottom* [[electron energy level]] of an [[energy ladder]] is calleed the *ground state*.

Electrons which are in the ground state have the **lowest energy**. That is, the **most negative** number of [[electronvolt]]s.

An [[electron]] which is *not* in the ground state is known as an [[excited electron]].

| Question | Answer |
| --- | --- |
| Ground state | The bottom electron energy level of an energy ladder; electrons here have the lowest (most negative) energy. |
