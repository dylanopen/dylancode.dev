# Hyperbolic functions of zero

Knowing the definitions that we covered of [[sinh]], [[cosh]] and
[[tanh]], we're able to work out some exact values of the hyperbolic
functions - by substituting for $x$.

## $\sinh0=0$

Proof:
- $\sinh x=\frac{e^x-e^{-x}}2$
- $\sinh0=\frac{e^0-e^{-0}}2$
- $=\frac{1-1}2$ (anything to the [[power of 0]] is 1)
- $=\frac02$
- $=0$

## $\cosh0=1$

Proof:
- $\cosh x=\frac{e^x+e^{-x}}2$
- $\cosh0=\frac{e^0+e^{-0}}2$
- $=\frac{1+1}2$ (anything to the [[power of 0]] is 1)
- $=\frac22$
- $=1$

## $\tanh0=$

Proof:
- $\tanh x=\frac{e^x-e^{-x}}{e^x+e^{-x}}$
- $\tanh 0=\frac{e^0-e^{-0}}{e^0+e^{-0}}$
- $=\frac{1-1}{1+1}$ (anything to the [[power of 0]] is 1)
- $=\frac02$
- $=0$
