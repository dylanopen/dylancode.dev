# Electromagnetic spectrum

## Types of electromagnetic waves

The 7 types of electromagnetic waves are:
- Radio waves
- Microwaves
- Infrared waves
- Visible light
- Ultraviolet waves
- X-rays
- Gamma rays

## Uses of electromagnetic waves

Each type of wave has different uses. Some examples:
- Radio waves
  - Radio and television broadcasting
  - Cell phone communication
- Microwaves
  - Cooking food
  - Radar systems
- Infrared waves
  - Remote controls
  - Thermal imaging
  - Night vision
- Visible light
  - Well, seeing things!
- Ultraviolet waves
  - Sterilization
  - Tanning beds
- X-rays
  - Medical imaging
  - Security scanners
- Gamma rays
  - Cancer treatment
  - Sterilization of medical equipment

## Speed of electromagnetic waves

Electromagnetic waves travel at the [[speed of light]] - which
is about $3.00 \times 10^8$ meters per second in a vacuum.

This means that all EM waves, no matteer their [[wavelength]] / type, travel at the
same [[speed]] in the same medium.
