Number of standard deviations from the [[mean]]



## [[flashcards]]

| Question | Answer |
| --- | --- |
| What does $Z$ represent in the formula $Z = \frac{x - \mu}{\sigma}$? | The number of standard deviations a data point $x$ is from the mean $\mu$. |
| What percentage of data falls within one standard deviation of the mean in a normal distribution? | 68.2% of the data lies within $\mu \pm \sigma$, meaning $Z = \pm 1$. |
