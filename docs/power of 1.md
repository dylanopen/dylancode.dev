# Power of 1

Anything raised to the [[power]] of 1 is itself:

$$
a^1=a
$$

### Example: evaluate $17^1$

- $17$

### Example: solve for $x$: $x^1=12$

- $x=12$

### Example: solve for $n$: $k^n=k$

- Anything to the [[power]] of $1$ is itself...
- so $n=1$
