# Alloy density

- An [[alloy]] is a mix of two or more metals.
- We usually write it as a *percentage by volume* of each metal.
- The total mass is the [[mass]] of each part of the alloy, added together.
- The total volume is the [[volume]] of each part of the alloy, added together.
