# Proton specific charge

> The [[specific charge]] of a *[[proton]]* is $9.58\times10^7\,C\,kg^{-1]$

## Calculating specific charge

- We can calculate [[specific charge]] by doing $charge\div mass$.
- The *charge* of a [[proton]] is $1.6\times10^{-19}$
- The *mass* of a [[proton]] is $1.67\times10^{-27}$
- $\frac{1.6\times10^{-19}}{1.67\times10^{-27}}=9.58\times10^7$
