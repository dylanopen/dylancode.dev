# Electron specific charge

> The magnitude of the [[specific charge]] of a *[[electron]]* is $1.76\times10^{11}$

## Calculating specific charge

- We can calculate [[specific charge]] by doing $charge\div mass$.
- The *charge* of a [[proton]] is $1.6\times10^{-19}$
- The *mass* of a [[proton]] is $9.11\times10^{-31}$
- $\frac{1.6\times10^{-19}}{9.11\times10^{-31}}=1.76\times10^{11}$
