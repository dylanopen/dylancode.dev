# Positron

A *positron* is the [[antiparticle]] of an [[electron]].

That means it has:
- the same [[mass]] as an [[electron]]
- the opposite [[charge]] of an [[electron]] (positive)

When a positron meets an [[electron]], they [[annihilation|annihilate]] each other, producing two [[photon]]s.

| Question | Answer |
| --- | --- |
| What is a positron? | The antiparticle of an electron. |
