# Types of wave

There are two main groups of waves:
- [[transverse wave|Transverse waves]]
- [[longitudinal wave|Longitudinal waves]]

They are distinguished by the *direction of oscillation* (vibration) relative to
the direction of energy transfer.

| **Question / Key Term** | **Answer / Definition** |
