# Specific charge

> The *specific charge* of a [[subatomic-particle]] is the ratio of its [[charge]] to its [[mass]].

In other words, how much charge it has for every unit of mass.

## Calculating specific charge

We can use this formula to calculate the specific charge of a particle:

$$
\text{Specific charge} = \frac{\text{Charge}}{\text{Mass}}
$$

Sometimes, we represent specific charge as just $\frac Qm$ because of its formula.

Where:
- $Q$ is the charge of the particle (in coulombs)
- $m$ is the mass of the particle (in kilograms)
