# Electrical power

## Calculating power

There are loads of different ways we can calculate the [[power]]:

$$
P=\frac{\Delta E}{\Delta t}
$$

$$
P=IV
$$

$$
P=I^2R
$$

$$
P=\frac{V^2}{R}
$$

## [[flashcards]]

| Question | Answer |
| --- | --- |
| What is the formula for power in terms of energy and time? | $P=\frac{\Delta E}{\Delta t}$ |
| What is the formula for electrical power in terms of current and voltage? | $P=IV$ |
| How do you calculate electrical power using current and resistance? | $P=I^2R$ |
| How do you calculate electrical power using voltage and resistance? | $P=\frac{V^2}{R}$ |
