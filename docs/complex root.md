# Complex root

## Solving $z^n=w$

- There will be $n$ solutions.
- They'll form a **regular** $n$-sided polygon centred at the origin, if we draw it on an Argand diagram.

## Solve $z^2=4$

- $z=2$ or $z=-2$

## Solve $z^2=-4$

- $z=2$ or $z=-2$

## Solve $z^2=4$

- $z=2$ or $z=-2$
