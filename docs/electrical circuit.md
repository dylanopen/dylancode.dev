# Electrical circuit

- An electrical circuit is an **[[energy]] delivery system**.
- It takes energy from the [[cell]]/[[battery]] and 'drops it off' at the [[electrical component]]s (such as a [[filament lamp]]).


## [[flashcards]]

| Question | Answer |
| --- | --- |
| An electrical circuit is what kind of system? | It is an **energy delivery system**. |
| What is the role of a cell or battery in a circuit? | It takes energy and 'drops it off' at the electrical components (such as a filament lamp). |
