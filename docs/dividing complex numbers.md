# Dividing complex numbers

To divide by a [[complex number]], we can first make the denominator *real*.

## Making the denominator real

We can make the denominator real by multiplying by the [[complex conjugate]] and
simplifying.

## [[flashcards]]

| Question | Answer |
| --- | --- |
| How do you make the denominator real when dividing complex numbers? | Multiply the numerator and denominator by the complex conjugate of the denominator, then simplify. |
