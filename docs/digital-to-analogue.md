# Digital to analogue conversion

If we have a digital signal, we can convert it into an analogue signal using a
process called *digital to analogue conversion* - using a DAC.

This basically converts a digital signal into an analogue signal by
*interpolating* the digital signal at regular intervals. It will take a series
of 1s and 0s, and create an analogue wave that approximates the digital signal.

## Why convert to analogue?

The main use is for sound. When you listen to music, you're hearing an analogue
signal - the sound waves in the air.
