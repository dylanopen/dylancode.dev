# Weak nuclear force

The *weak nuclear force* is one of the [[particle forces]].

> The weak force manages [[radioactive decay]].

## Range

- The weak nuclear [[force]] has the *shortest [[range]]* of all the [[particle forces]].
- Its [[range]] is about $1\times10^{18}\,m$.
