# Specific latent heat

The specific latent heat (either of [[fusion]] or [[vaporisation]]) is the energy required to change the state of 1 kg of a substance.

## Equation

> $$
> Q=mL
> $$

- $Q$ is the [[thermal energy]] added / released
- $m$ is the [[mass]] of the substance that [[state change|changed state]].
- $L$ is the specific latent heat, see below for the types.

## Types of specific latent heat

- [[specific latent heat of fusion]], involving a state change between a [[solid]] and [[liquid]].
- [[specific latent heat of vaporisation]], involving a state change between a [[liquid]] and [[gas]].
