# Gas volume & pressure

- The higher the [[volume]] of a gas, the lower its [[pressure]].
    - This assumes that the other [[gas properties]] (temperature and mass) remain constant.
- It is an [[inverse proportion]] relationship.
- This is called Boyle's law

## $P\times V=\text{constant}$

## $P_1 V_1=P_2 V_2$

- Where:
    - $P_1$ is the initial pressure of the gas
    - $V_1$ is the initial volume of the gas
    - $P_2$ is the final pressure of the gas
    - $V_2$ is the final volume of the gas
- If we know three out of four of these, we can find the other one.
