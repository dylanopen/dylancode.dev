# Conic section transformations

There are a few different transformations we can apply to a [[conic-section]].

## Parabola translations

Whenever a [[parabola]] is [translated](translation), we can just replace $x$ with $x-h$ and $y$ with $y-k$, where $(h,k)$ is the translation vector.

### Translate the parabola $y^2=4ax$ by the vector $(h, k)$ and find its new focus.

- Replace $x$ with $x-h$ and $y$ with $y-k$:
  - $(y-k)^2=4a(x-h)$
- The original focus was at $(a,0)$
- It's translated by $(h, k)$, so the new focus is at $(a+h, k)$.

### Translate the parabola $y^2=8x$ by the vector $(9, -8)$ and find its new focus

- Replace $x$ with $x-9$ and $y$ with $y+8$:
  - $(y+8)^2=4a(x-9)$
- The original vertex was at $(0,0)$
- It's translated by $(9, -8)$, so the new vertex is at $(9,-8)$.

- The original focus was at $(2,0)$
- It's translated by $(9, -8)$, so the new focus is at $(11, -8)$.
