# sinh

$\sinh$, or **hyperbolic sine**, is defined like this:

$$
\sinh x=\frac{e^x-e^{-x}}2
$$

> It's pronounced 'shine' or 'cinch'.

## Finding from $\tanh$

Using the definition for $\tanh$:

$$
\tanh=\frac{\sinh}{\cosh}
$$

...we can deduce that:

$$
\sinh=\cosh\cdot\tanh
$$
