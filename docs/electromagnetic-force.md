# Electromagnetic force

The EM force is the [[force]] which acts between [[particle]]s with a [[charge]].

It helps to keep the [[electron]]s in orbit around the [[nucleus]].

## Strength

- It's the second strongest of the 4 [[particle-forces]].
- The strength is inversely proportional to the **square** of the **distance** between the [[particle]]s or [[matter]].

## Range

- The electromagnetic force has effectively an infinite range.
- It can act over very large distances, but it's usually only significant at the atomic level (when the distance is really small)
