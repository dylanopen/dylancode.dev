# Fusion

**Fusion** is when a **state change** occurs, involving a [[solid]] turning into a [[liquid]], or a [[liquid]] turning into a [[solid]].

The [[specific latent heat of fusion]] is the [[specific latent heat]] for a state change between solid and liquid.
