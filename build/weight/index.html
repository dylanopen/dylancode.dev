Weight

Weight is the force in which the earth attracts an object.

W=mg

  • W is the weight of the object, in newtons (N)
  • m is the mass of the object, in kilograms (kg)
  • g is the acceleration due to gravity, in metres per second (ms^-2, see acceleration).

Increasing mass

A greater force is needed in order to attract an object with more mass.

This means that objects with more mass will have more weight, in the same gravitational field.
