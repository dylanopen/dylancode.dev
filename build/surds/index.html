Surds

A surd is an expression that includes a root, such as a square root or cube root. Surds are often used to represent irrational numbers.

There are some operations we can do on surds, including:

  • simplifying surds
  • adding surds
  • subtracting surds
  • multiplying surds
  • dividing surds
  • rationalising the denominator
