Quadratic roots and coefficients

The roots in polynomials are closely linked to the coefficients of that polynomial.

All polynomials in this chapter will be written as follows - the letters are important here:

ax^2+bx+c

Sum of roots

If p and q are roots of ax^2+bx+c=0, then p+q=-\frac ba.

This means that the sum of roots of a quadratic is equal to -\frac ba.

Find the sum of the roots of 2x^2-5x+3=0

  • The sum of roots is -\frac ba:
  • =-\frac{-5}2
  • =\frac52
  • Answer: sum of roots = \frac52

Find the sum of the roots of x^2+4x+4=0

  • The sum of roots is -\frac ba:
  • =-\frac41
  • =-4
  • Answer: sum of roots = -4

The sum of roots of the polynomial 3x^2+kx-9=0 is 7. Find the value of k.

  • a=3, b=k, c=-9
  • The sum of roots is 7, which represents -\frac ba:
    • 7=-\frac k3
    • k=-21
  • Answer: k=-21

The sum of roots of the polynomial 2x^2+2mx+3=0 is -3. Find the value of m.

  • a=2, b=2m, c=3
  • The sum of roots is -3, which represents -\frac ba:
    • -3=-\frac{2m}2
    • -3=-m
    • m=3
  • Answer: m=3

Product of roots

If p and q are roots of ax^2+bx+c=0, then p\times q=\frac ca (positive).

In words, the product of roots of a quadratic is equal to \frac ca.

Find the product of the roots of 2x^2-5x+3=0

  • The product of roots is \frac ca:
  • =\frac32
  • Answer: product of roots = \frac32

Find the product of the roots of x^2+4x+4=0

  • The product of roots is \frac ca:
  • =\frac44
  • =1
  • Answer: product of roots = 1

The product of roots of the polynomial kx^2+10x+4 is \frac23. Find the value of k.

  • a=k, b=10, c=4
  • The product of roots is \frac23, which represents \frac ca:
    • \frac23=\frac4k
    • 2k=12
    • k=6
  • Answer: k=6

Evaluating other expressions

The equation x^2+5x+4=0 has roots p and q. Evaluate p^2+q^2

  • The only things we know about p and q are:
    • p+q=-\frac ba=-5
    • pq=\frac ca=4
  • We need to rewrite p^2+q^2 in terms of p+q and pq only:
    • p^2+q^2=(p+q)^2-2pq
  • Now we can substitute the values we know in!
    • =(-5)^2-2(4)
    • =25-8
    • =17
  • Answer: p^2+q^2=17
