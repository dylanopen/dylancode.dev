Double slit diffraction

w=\frac{\lambda D}s

Where:

  • w is the distance between the fringes (diffracted peaks)
  • \lambda is the wavelength
  • D is the distance from the screen with the gratings to the projected screen
  • s is the distance between the slits (gratings)
