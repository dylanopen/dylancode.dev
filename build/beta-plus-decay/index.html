Beta plus decay

In beta minus decay, beta minus radiation is emitted from the nucleus, and it has the equation:

^A_ZX\,\rightarrow\,\,^A_{Z-1}Y\,\,+\,\,^0_{+1}\beta\,\,+\,\,\nu_e

  • ^0_{+1}e is a positron - it’s a positive electron.
