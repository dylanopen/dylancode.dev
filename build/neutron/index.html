Neutron

Neutron mass: 1.675\times10^{-27}kg
Neutron charge: 0

Location

  • Neutrons are found in the nucleus of an atoms.
  • They’re found alongside the proton.

Charge

  • Neutrons have no charge - they are neutral.
  • Their charge is 0.

Mass

  • Neutrons have a tiny bit more mass than protons and are way heavier than an electron.
  • Mass of a neutron: 1.675\times10^{-27}
  • Its relative mass is about 1/1836 of a proton.
