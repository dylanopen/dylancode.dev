Planck constant

The Planck constant describes how the energy of a photon is related to its frequency.

h=6.63 \times 10^{-34}\,\,Js

We write it with the letter h.

Uses

We use planck’s constant in lots of equations, such as:

  • The photoelectric equation - E_{kmax} = hf - \phi
  • The equation for the energy of a photon - E = hf
  • The equation linking threshold frequency and work function - \phi = hf_0
