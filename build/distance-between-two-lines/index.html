Distance between two lines

Find the distance between two lines

  • l_1\Rightarrow r_1=\pmatrix{1\\0\\0}+\lambda\pmatrix{0\\1\\1}

  • l_2\Rightarrow r_2=\pmatrix{-1\\3\\-1}+\mu\pmatrix{2\\-1\\-1}

  • Let A equal the point at which AB intersects with l_1

  • Let B equal the point at which AB intersects with l_2

  • \vec{OA}=\pmatrix{1\\\lambda\\\lambda}

  • \vec{OB}=\pmatrix{-1+2\mu\\3-\mu}

  • \vec{AB}=\vec{OB}-\vec{OA}=\pmatrix{-2+2\mu\\3-\mu-\lambda\\-1-\mu-\lambda}

  • d_1=\pmatrix{0\\1\\1}

  • d_2=\pmatrix{2\\-1\\-1}

  • \vec{AB}\cdot d_1=0

  • \pmatrix{-2+2\mu\\3-\mu-\lambda\\-1-\mu-lambda}\cdot\pmatrix{0\\1\\1}=0

  • \vec{AB}\cdot d_2=0

  • \pmatrix{-2+2\mu\\3-\mu-\lambda\\-1-\mu-lambda}\cdot\pmatrix{2\\-1\\-1}=0
