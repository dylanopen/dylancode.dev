Expectation of sum

If we want to find the expectation of the sum of two random variable, for example, X and Y, we can just add the individual expectations:

E(X+Y)=E(X)+E(Y)

X and Y are random variables: X has an expectation of 5, and Y has a mean value of 7. Find E(X+Y)

  • We know that E(X+Y)=E(X)+E(Y).
  • E(X)=5
  • E(Y)=7
  • E(X+Y)=E(X)+E(Y)
  • E(X+Y)=5+7=12
