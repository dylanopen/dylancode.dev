Stress

  • Stress is the force applied per unit area, similar to pressure.
  • We can write it as \sigma.
  • Its unit are force divided by time, so newtons per metre squared (Nm^{-2}).

Stress equation

\sigma=\frac FA

  • \sigma is the —, in Nm^{-2}.
  • F is the force, in N,
  • A is the area, in m^2.
