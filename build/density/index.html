Density

Density measures the amount of mass per unit volume.

Equation

\text{density}=\frac{\text{mass}}{\text{volume}}

\rho=\frac mV

  • Density is measured in kilograms per metre cubed (kg\,m^{-3})
  • mass is measured in kilograms (kg)
  • volume is measured in metres cubed (m^3)

Units

  • The SI unit for density is kg\,m^{-3}.
  • We can also use g\,cm^{-3}.
