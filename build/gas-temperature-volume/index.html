**# Gas temperature & volume (Charles’ law)

In an ideal gas:

The volume of a gas is directly proportional to its absolute temperature

Formulae

We can write Charles’ law in two ways:

\frac VT=\text{constant}

  • V is the volume of the gas
  • T is the temperature of the gas in kelvin (absolute temperature)

\frac{V_1}{T_1}=\frac{V_2}{T_2}
