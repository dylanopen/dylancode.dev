Gas temperature, volume & pressure

Knowing the laws for:

  • **# Gas temperature & volume (Charles’ law)
  • gas temperature & pressure (pressure law)
  • gas volume & pressure

We can derive a formula.

\frac{PV}T=\text{constant}

  • P is the pressure of the gas (see gas pressure)
  • V is the volume of the gas (see gas volume)
  • T is the temperature of the gas (see gas temperature)

\frac{P_1V_1}{T_1}=\frac{P_2V_2}{T_2}

  • P_1 is the pressure of the gas initially (see gas pressure)
  • V_1 is the volume of the gas initially (see gas volume)
  • T_1 is the temperature of the gas initially (see gas temperature)
  • P_2 is the pressure of the gas at the end (see gas pressure)
  • V_2 is the volume of the gas at the end (see gas volume)
  • T_2 is the temperature of the gas at the end (see gas temperature)
