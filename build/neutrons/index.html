Neutrons

Location

  • Neutrons are found in the nucleus of an atom.
  • They’re found alongside the protons.

Charge

  • Neutrons have no charge - they are neutral.
  • Their charge relative to other subatomic particles is 0.

Mass

  • The atomic mass of a neutron is 1.
  • This is (almost) the same as the mass of a proton, which is also 1.
  • It is much larger than an electron.
