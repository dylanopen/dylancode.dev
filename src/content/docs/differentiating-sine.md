---
title: Differentiating sine
---

> If $y=a\sin bx$, then $\frac{dy}{dx}=ab\cos bx$

### Find $\frac{dy}{dx}$ of $y=2\sin x$

- $\frac{dy}{dx}=2\cos x$

### Find $\frac{dy}{dx}$ of $y=\sin3x$

- $\frac{dy}{dx}=3\cos3x$

### Find $\frac{dy}{dx}$ of $y=2\sin5x$

- $\frac{dy}{dx}=2(5\cos5x)$
- $\frac{dy}{dx}=10\cos5x$

### Find $\frac{dy}{dx}$ of $y=-3\sin(-2x)$

- $\frac{dy}{dx}=-3(-2\cos(-2x))$
- $\frac{dy}{dx}=6\cos(-2x)$

### Find $\frac{dy}{dx}$ of $y=10\sin\frac x2$

- $\frac{dy}{dx}=10(\frac x2\cos(\frac x2))$
- $\frac{dy}{dx}=5x\cos\frac x2$

### Find $\frac{dy}{dx}$ of $y=\tan4x\cdot\cos4x$

- $y=\frac{\sin4x}{\cos4x}\cdot\cos4x$
- $y=\sin4x$
- $\frac{dy}{dx}=4\cos4x$
