title: Surds
---

A **surd** is an expression that includes a root, such as a square root or cube
root. Surds are often used to represent irrational numbers.

There are some operations we can do on surds, including:
- [simplifying surds](/simplifying-surds)
- [adding surds](/adding-surds)
- [subtracting surds](/subtracting-surds)
- [multiplying surds](/multiplying-surds)
- [dividing surds](/dividing-surds)
- [rationalising the denominator](/rationalising-the-denominator)
