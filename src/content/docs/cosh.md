title: cosh
---

$\cosh$, or **hyperbolic cosine**, is defined like this:

$$
\cosh x=\frac{e^x+e^{-x}}2
$$

> It's pronounced 'cosh' - as it's spelt.

## Finding from $\tanh$

Using the definition for $\tanh$:

$$
\tanh=\frac{\sinh}{\cosh}
$$

...we can deduce that:

$$
\cosh=\frac{\sinh}{\tanh}
$$
