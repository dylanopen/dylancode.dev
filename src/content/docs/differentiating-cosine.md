---
title: Differentiating cosine
---

> If $y=a\cos bx$, then $\frac{dy}{dx}=-ab\sin ax$

### Find $\frac{dy}{dx}$ of $y=\frac13\cos x$

- $\frac{dy}{dx}=-\frac13\sin x$

### Find $\frac{dy}{dx}$ of $y=\cos2x$

- $\frac{dy}{dx}=-2\sin2x$

### Find $\frac{dy}{dx}$ of $y=-\cos7x$

- $\frac{dy}{dx}=7\sin7x$

### Find $\frac{dy}{dx}$ of $y=2\cos4x$

- $\frac{dy}{dx}=-8\sin4x$
